Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How long does it take to get a quote?

Most vendors provide instant quotes through our system within 30 seconds to 2 minutes. For standard orders (uniforms, banners, t-shirts), you'll see pricing immediately after uploading your design. For complex projects (large format printing, custom packaging, specialty items), vendors may request additional details like dimensions, material specifications, or color samples. Typical response time for custom quotes is 2-4 hours during business hours (8am-6pm EAT). Rush quote requests are processed within 1 hour. All quotes include itemized pricing for materials, setup, labor, and any applicable rush fees.

‚ùì What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major design file formats: PDF, Adobe Illustrator (AI), Photoshop (PSD), InDesign (INDD), Corel Draw (CDR), EPS, SVG, JPG, PNG, GIF, and BMP. PDF is our recommended format as it preserves all fonts and design elements. Our AI file-check system automatically scans uploaded files for: resolution (minimum 300 DPI for print), color mode (RGB vs CMYK), missing fonts, and oversized dimensions. If issues are detected, you'll receive specific recommendations before submitting to the vendor. Most vendors can work with native files, but PDF ensures best results. File size limit is 100MB per upload.

‚ùì Is M-Pesa payment secure?

Yes, absolutely. Our M-Pesa integration uses bank-level encryption (SSL 256-bit) and has been sandbox-tested for security. We do not store your M-Pesa PIN or phone number credentials‚Äîyou authenticate directly with Safaricom's secure servers. All transactions are PCI-DSS compliant. Your personal and payment data are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We also offer card payments (Visa/Mastercard), bank transfers, and manual M-Pesa deposits as alternatives. Each transaction generates an invoice and receipt for your records. Refunds are processed within 2-3 business days to your original payment method.

‚ùì Can I track my order in real-time?

Yes! Once you place an order and payment is confirmed, your order enters our tracking system. You can monitor it through 5 stages: File Review ‚Üí Design Approval ‚Üí Production ‚Üí Quality Check ‚Üí Ready for Collection/Delivery. Each stage shows estimated time remaining. For delivery orders, GPS tracking is available and you'll receive notifications via SMS and email at each milestone. Vendors can also send custom updates‚Äîfor example, "Your design is approved, printing starts tomorrow." You can message the vendor directly from the SmartPrint Pro dashboard with questions or modification requests, and they'll respond within 2 hours during business hours.

‚ùì What if I'm not happy with my order?

Each vendor on SmartPrint Pro maintains a satisfaction guarantee. If your printed order doesn't meet expectations‚Äîquality issues, color mismatch, wrong dimensions, or design errors‚Äîcontact the vendor within 48 hours of receiving your order. Document the issue with photos if applicable. Most vendors will: (1) offer a reprint at no cost, (2) provide partial refund, or (3) arrange replacement. If vendor and customer cannot resolve the issue, SmartPrint Pro mediates and helps facilitate a resolution. Vendor reputation scores include satisfaction ratings from past customers, so you can see how well they handle issues before ordering. Over 97% of disputes are resolved to customer satisfaction within 5 business days.

‚ùì Can I get rush orders?

Yes! When requesting a quote, you can select your preferred timeline: Standard (3-5 days), Urgent (1-2 days), or Express (same-day or next-day). Rush pricing varies by vendor and order complexity, but typically adds 15-50% to the base cost. For example: standard t-shirt printing KES 2,000 might be KES 2,500 for urgent or KES 3,000 for express. Some vendors specialize in rush services and maintain spare capacity. Express orders (same-day printing) are available for items like banners, flyers, and t-shirts from vendors in Nairobi and Mombasa. Note: Design approval still takes 1-2 hours even on rush orders‚Äîvendors need to verify your design before printing. Emergency requests should be communicated directly to the vendor.

‚ùì Is there a minimum order quantity?

Minimums vary widely by vendor and service type. Some vendors specialize in single-item orders (1 t-shirt, 1 mug, 1 business card pack), while others focus on bulk printing and have minimums of 50-100+ units. Our vendor filters let you search for "single-item" or "low-minimum" services. Typical minimums: Stickers (10 units), Business cards (50), T-shirts (1 or 5 depending on vendor), Banners (1), Mugs (1), Uniforms (5), Branded packaging (100+). When you get quotes, each vendor will clearly state their minimum quantities. If you need just 1 or 2 items, filter by vendors offering "short-run printing" or "on-demand services" in Nairobi or Mombasa.

‚ùì Do you deliver to all areas of Kenya?

Most vendors deliver within Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret. Some offer nationwide delivery for an additional flat fee (typically KES 500-2,000 depending on distance). Delivery times: Same-day or next-day within Nairobi CBD, 1-2 days to major cities, 2-5 days to rural areas. Pickup is always free at vendor locations during business hours (8am-6pm). For areas outside major cities, you can arrange via: (1) vendors' delivery partners (added cost), (2) your own courier service, or (3) coordination with the vendor for alternative methods. When requesting a quote, specify your location and we'll show you vendors who can serve you. All delivery fees are transparent and quoted upfront before you order.
